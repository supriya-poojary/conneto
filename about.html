Our Story

We Exist to Close the Opportunity Gap

Founded in Udupi, Karnataka, Conneto was born from a real frustration — talented students struggling to find the right internships, and companies spending weeks on irrelevant applications.

We built a platform that connects the right people with the right opportunities, with tools that make the process transparent, trackable, and genuinely useful for both sides.

Mission & Vision

The beliefs and goals that drive every decision we make at Conneto.

Our Mission
Simplify. Connect. Launch.
To simplify the internship hiring process by creating a transparent, efficient, and accessible platform for students and recruiters — removing every layer of friction and creating genuine connections that matter. We measure success not by traffic, but by careers launched and talent discovered.
Transparency at every application stage
Accessibility for students across all backgrounds
Efficiency for recruiters and hiring teams
Our Vision
India's Most Trusted Internship Platform
To become the most trusted internship ecosystem in India — a platform where every student finds a launchpad that fits their potential, and every company discovers talent that powers their next chapter. We envision a world where geography and background are never barriers to opportunity.
