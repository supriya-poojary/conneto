Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Most common questions answered — saves you from writing that long email.

01
How can students apply for internships on Conneto?

Create a free profile, upload your resume, and browse internships matching your skills. Apply with one click and track your application status in real time from your personal dashboard. You are notified instantly at every stage.

02
How do companies shortlist and contact candidates?

Companies review all applicants, browse detailed student profiles and resumes, and use smart filters to sort by skills, GPA, location, and experience. Shortlisted students are notified instantly and invited for interviews directly through the platform.

03
Is registration completely free for students?

Yes — completely free, always. Students can create a profile, upload a resume, apply to internships, and track all applications without any cost. We believe every student deserves fair access to great career opportunities.

04
How do I reset my account password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your registered email. A secure reset link arrives within a few minutes. If you face any trouble, contact support@conneto.in and we will resolve it within the hour.
